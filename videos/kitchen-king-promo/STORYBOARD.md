## Video direction

- palette system: maroon/ink grounds for dark frames (1,2,6), cream canvas for proof frame (4); brand red for display type accents; amber for glows, chips, and particle field; never pure #000/#fff.
- motion grammar: VO-paced reveals on long-tail power3; each piece enters on its spoken cue, back-half reveals preferred; holds are still with at-most subtle particle drift; no lazy breathing, no back-half camera pans.
- rhythm: Frame 3 (Appetite) and Frame 5 (Legacy) are the breathers — one slow move each, then held reads. Frames 1, 4 are punch beats; 2 and 6 are hero beats.
- negative list: no bokeh/purple AI gradients, no browser chrome, no stock confetti; failure modes forbidden — slideshow (front-load-then-freeze) and screensaver (everything floating).
